Skills for Communicating Information (IT007UN)

Course Overview

Effective communication is a powerful skill that can help you achieve your objectives, whether presenting to one person or one thousand. This course aims to enhance your communication skills by enabling you to focus on your goals, understand your audience, and convey your message with clarity and impact.

Course Learning Objectives

Upon the successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

  1. Understand how to communicate effectively and efficiently.
  2. Develop the problem solving, study and communication skills needed when tackling both conventional and coursework-based examinations.
  3. Develop skills for future careers such as applying for a job, participating in a job interview, collaborating with colleagues, telecommunicating, and so on.
